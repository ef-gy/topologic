Welcome to Topologic/V10!

Topologic is your versatile, open-source window into the fourth dimension and beyond.

Click or tap anywhere outside this panel to get back to the application. Drag the mouse or pan to rotate in the currently active dimension; scroll to zoom in or out.

Select the dimension to rotate in - and a host of other settings - in the left-hand Settings menu.

Information

Get the source code at https://github.com/ef-gy/topologic.

Read the full documentation.

Support the project by getting the iOS app or the OSX app in their respective app store.

Embedding

Feel free to embed the output of this application in your presentations, blog articles or other web content. Click on Export/Import... to generate SVGs, links or HTML code to embed this application directly in your site.

The Export/Import... button is also available from the left-hand Settings menu.
